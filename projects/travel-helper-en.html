Side Project · CASE STUDY

Universal Travel Helper

0→1 strategy exploration for an AI travel-planning product: from market opportunity, competitive gaps, and user insight to positioning and business goals — then converging it all into an executable MVP.

Concept Product Strategy Market research Competitive analysis MRD · Personas

Market OpportunityMarket opportunity

Travel is shifting from group tours to highly personalised independent travel. Travellers increasingly rely on digital tools for planning and real-time decisions, yet existing tools share clear pain points: fragmented information, hard to filter, shallow personalisation, and no real-time adaptability in-trip. Online travel keeps growing, and AI-driven customisation is the new growth frontier — that's the moment to enter.

Pain & GapPain points & market gaps

I laid out the existing solutions to find what current tools can't do, and mapped those gaps to the product's unique selling points (USPs).

PersonasTarget users

The Efficiency-Seeker · Sarah

30 · Project Manager, tech

"I love travelling but hate the homework! I want someone to just tell me the smoothest, best-value way to go — ideally auto-adjusting too."

Values efficiency, tired of comparing across many sites, wants to spend time enjoying the trip rather than on tedious detail.

The Cautious Traveller · David

45 · Accountant

"I care about budget; with so many pass types it's hard to know which is worth it. Unexpected situations abroad make me anxious — I want a safety net."

Budget-sensitive, values verifiable, transparent information, and fears complex pass rules leading to a bad/uneconomical purchase.

Positioning · USPProduct positioning

  • Deep personalisation & real-time in-trip optimisation — smart itinerary adjustments based on live changes (weather, transit, state), like a personal concierge.
  • Seamless support & peace of mind — integrated translation, local ticketing, live budgeting, and emergency guidance in one "safety net".
  • Authoritative, real-time information & verification — key info kept authoritative and fresh, with changes auto-flagged to build trust.
  • Smart financial transparency & value maximisation — intelligently compares the real value of tickets and passes to find the most economical choice.

Business GoalsBusiness goals

Short-term (12 months)

  • First-month active users 500K, quarterly MAU growth 20%+
  • Month-1 retention 40%; smart-itinerary adoption 60%
  • Validate an initial monetisation model

Long-term (3–5 years)

  • Become the leading AI travel-planning brand in Asia
  • Tens of millions of active users; NPS 60+
  • Stable positive cash flow and sustainable profitability

Strategy → MVPFrom strategy to MVP

The full vision was too large to ship at once as a solo PM. Using the CRISPE structure, I evaluated options on "user value × feasibility × cost" and converged on the first slice that solves a high-value pain point precisely and is controllable to ship — Ticket & Pass Comparison.
